Remarks

The Skyfather archetype. Ruler, protector, enforcer of cosmic order.

Thunder-wielder, king of gods, patriarchal sovereignty.

Jupiter in Roman myth is the god of the sky and thunder, protector of the state and law.

Zeus in Greek mythology overthrows Kronos, reigns from Olympus, and commands lightning.

In Egypt, Amun-Ra combines creation, kingship, and solar divinity.

Thor embodies the working-class thunder god, loyal and furious.

Babylonian Marduk is a dragon-slaying champion, appointed by the gods as ruler of all.

Fuxi in Chinese lore is a culture-bringer, compass-maker, and cosmic regulator.

Associated with the planet Jupiter and the weekday Thursday.
